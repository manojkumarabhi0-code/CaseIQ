# CaseIQ
CaseIQ: Opportunity Intelligence Engine is an AI-powered system that evaluates EB1A client profiles, generates a case strength score, identifies gaps, and recommends actionable strategies to improve eligibility, enabling faster qualification, stronger cases, and higher conversion efficiency.
## :rocket: What it does

CaseIQ is an AI-powered intelligence platform that analyzes EB-1A profiles and provides structured insights, gap analysis, and strategy suggestions. It helps users better understand their profile strengths and areas for improvement based on publicly available criteria.

---

## :dart: Who it's for

* Individuals exploring EB-1A eligibility
* Professionals seeking structured self-assessment
* Immigration attorneys and consultants (as a support tool)

---

## :bulb: Problem it solves

Understanding EB-1A requirements can be complex and unclear. CaseIQ simplifies this by organizing publicly available information into structured insights, helping users evaluate their profiles and identify potential areas for improvement.

---

## :gear: Features

* Profile analysis based on EB-1A criteria
* Gap identification with general recommendations
* Structured insights for better decision-making
* User-friendly interface for profile evaluation
* Secure handling of user-submitted data
